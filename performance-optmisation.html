What we tune

Every layer of the database performance stack.

Query & Plan Tuning

Execution plan analysis, hint strategy, query rewrites, plan stability and plan freezing across all major engines.

Index Strategy

Compound, partial, covering, filtered and function-based indexes — including ruthless removal of unused ones.

Memory & Buffer Tuning

SGA/PGA, buffer pool, work mem, sort areas and cache hit ratios — sized correctly for your real workload.

Storage & I/O

Tablespace layout, partitioning, compression, storage tier selection and async I/O configuration.

Concurrency & Locking

Isolation level review, deadlock analysis, blocking-chain identification and connection-pool tuning.

Monitoring & Alerting

Prometheus, Grafana, OEM, DPA dashboards with proactive alerting on regression — before users feel it.

Frequently asked questions

How quickly will I see improvements?

Most engagements deliver measurable quick wins in the first week. A typical health check identifies 10–20 prioritized fixes; we usually implement the top 3–5 live during the assessment.

What databases do you tune?

Oracle, SQL Server, PostgreSQL, MySQL, MariaDB, Aurora (both engines), Azure SQL, and MongoDB. We have specialists for each.

Will tuning break my application?

Every change is reversible and tested in non-production first. We never apply plan changes, index changes or parameter changes to prod without your approval and a rollback plan.

Do you tune production systems live?

Yes, when needed — particularly for P1 incidents. We use plan baselines, query store and similar mechanisms to make changes safely on running systems.

What happens after the engagement?

You get a documented playbook, dashboards and a knowledge-transfer session. Many clients keep us on a light monthly retainer to catch regressions early.
