Frequently asked questions

Should I lift-and-shift or re-platform?

It depends on your timeline, total cost of ownership goals and how much your application coupling allows. Our assessment phase models both scenarios so you can choose with data, not gut feel.

How do you keep costs under control?

FinOps is built into every engagement. We model reserved capacity, savings plans, autoscaling triggers and storage tiers up-front, then run monthly cost reviews post-migration.

Will the application need code changes?

For lift-and-shift, usually no. For re-platform (e.g., Oracle → PostgreSQL), we use AWS SCT, Babelfish or custom mappers to minimize changes — and quantify them in the design phase.

How do you handle data sovereignty and compliance?

Region selection, encryption keys, audit logging and access boundaries are designed against your specific regulatory frameworks (GDPR, HIPAA, FINRA, etc.) from day one.

What's the typical downtime?

For most engagements: zero. We use CDC-based parallel-run and DNS cutover. Where downtime is unavoidable, we plan and announce it within agreed maintenance windows.
