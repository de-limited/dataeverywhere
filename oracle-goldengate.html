Frequently asked questions

Do you support GoldenGate Microservices Architecture (MA)?

Yes — we deploy MA exclusively for greenfield engagements and have a proven migration path from Classic to MA for existing estates.

What's the largest GG deployment you've worked on?

A global bank running 40+ active-active database pairs across 6 regions, replicating ~120M transactions/day with sub-second lag.

Can GoldenGate replicate to non-Oracle targets?

Yes. GG for Big Data and GG Heterogeneous support PostgreSQL, SQL Server, MySQL, Kafka, Snowflake, BigQuery, Hadoop, Elastic and more.

How do you handle replication failures or trail corruption?

Our runbooks cover every documented failure mode. We carry pre-built recovery scripts and have a 15-minute SLA for P1 incidents.

Will you train our internal team?

Always. Knowledge transfer is part of every engagement — we don't believe in creating dependency.

GoldenGate troubles? Or a new deployment?

Talk to a certified GG engineer within 24 hours. No sales pitch — just engineering.
