Frequently asked questions

How long does a typical integration project take?

Most point-to-point integrations go live in 4–8 weeks. Enterprise-wide data fabric programs typically run as 3–6 month phased engagements with incremental value delivered every sprint.

Can you integrate without disrupting production?

Yes. We use log-based CDC, blue/green deployments and parallel-run validation so source systems continue serving traffic throughout the cutover. Zero-downtime is our default operating mode.

Do you work with legacy mainframes?

Absolutely. We have deep experience exposing IBM Db2 for z/OS, AS/400 and VSAM data through modern APIs and CDC streams without requiring application changes.

Will I be locked into a specific tool?

No. We design with open standards (Kafka, Avro, JSON Schema, dbt) so your pipeline can evolve as your stack changes.

How do you handle sensitive data (PII/PHI)?

Masking, tokenisation and policy-based access control are built into the pipeline. We align with GDPR, HIPAA, PCI-DSS and SOC 2 requirements from day one.
